ACT NOW, ACT BOLDLY!


We, the Environmental Philippines Organization believes that climate change needs behavior change; all human beings should be part of the solution rather than being part of the problem, join the fight and we should do what is right. Together we take ACTION.

Conserve Water

The less water you use, the less runoff and wastewater that eventually and up in the ocean.



Don’t send chemicals into our water ways

Choose nontoxic chemicals in the home and office.



Plant a Tree

Tree give food and oxygen and they help save energy, clean the air, and help fight climate change.



Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Refuse, Repurpose

Cut down on what you throw away and follow the five “5R’s” to conserve natural resources and landfill space.
