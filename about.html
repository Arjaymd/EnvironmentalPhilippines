About Us

Environmental Philippines Organization

The Philippines is a wonderful country, full of natural wonders, yet most of its natural resources are threatened by environmental issues. As a result, the Environmental Philippines was created, Environmental Philippines is an organization that was made by a group of Nationalian Students who are dedicated in bringing latest news about the environment in the Philippines. Moreover, the Environmental Philippines is always updating and disseminating information about the problems and challenges that our environment faces, as well as raising awareness about why we should care about our natural wonders and preserve its beauty.
